Carolina Students for Bernie

Carolina Students for Bernie is the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill branch of the Bernie Sanders presidential campaign. I joined the newly-formed organization back in September, utilizing an opportunity to develop their brand and logo as well as gain experience managing other designers. CSFB acts as the ground game for the Chapel Hill area, encouraging students, faculty, and city residents to participate in the democratic process.

I am currently building a website for the organization here.
