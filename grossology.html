Grossology Movie Poster

The UNC Morehead Planetarium and Science Center specializes in educational productions for children in their halfdome show. Grossology and You is a feature-length animated movie that I helped work on during my time as an intern for the Planetarium. Toward the end of my internship and just before production on the movie finished, I was asked to design a movie poster to advertise the future halfdome show that included all of the main characters in a scene from the movie. (Completed December 2014)
